About us

Red Winter Consultants Engineering is an independent organization of consultants under direct management of Ir. Yasumori Minori, M. Sc, established in 1981 with structural design as its core of business. Since then, the company has grown and expanded the line of services to include Soil Investigation and Surveying, Architectural, Structural, M/E Design, Civil and Infrastructure design, Value Engineering, Building Audit, Project Management/Construction Management, Special Projects, Mini Bored Piles and Mini Piles system, that gives the Clients the benefit of 1-stop engineering service. During 30 years of its contribution in construction business, the society has viewed Red Winter as a 'workshop' that is always ready to solve any building problems or planning their new projects. By maintaining a solid and full of innovation team work, Red Winter always strive to give its best service and high dedication, which has result in large number of return clients.
